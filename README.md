# Global Financial Dataset for Tableau Visualization

This dataset is derived from Tableau training resources and was used for educational visualization exercises.  
It models a global business operation with interconnected data tables — *Countries, Suppliers, Products, Orders, and Customers* — to analyze financial performance across multiple regions.

*Dataset Structure:*
- Countries: Country name, region, and geographical area.
- Suppliers: Supplier names, countries, and IDs.
- Products: Product category, cost, and pricing details.
- Orders: Quantity, revenue, and order date.
- Customers: Customer identifiers and associated order data.

*Purpose:*
The dataset supports exercises in Tableau focusing on supplier contribution, revenue analysis, cost efficiency, and global sales trends.

> Originally prepared in 2024 as part of Tableau academic training material.  
> Updated for educational use in 2025.
